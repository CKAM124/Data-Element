# Quota Share Screen UI Design

## Overview
This document outlines the UI design for the Quota Share screen with Lead and Following views.

## Lead View
- **Header**: Quota Share - Lead View
- **Key Fields**:
  - Quota Percentage
  - Lead Underwriter
  - Contact Information
  - Terms and Conditions

- **Buttons**:
  - **Save**
  - **Cancel**
  
- **Layout**:
  - Fields displayed in a vertical form.
  - Use a card layout for clarity.

## Following View
- **Header**: Quota Share - Following View
- **Key Fields**:
  - Quota Percentage
  - Following Underwriter
  - Note Section for additional Information

- **Buttons**:
  - **Save**
  - **Cancel**
  
- **Layout**:
  - Similar card layout as the Lead view for consistency.
